About the Company

Provident Capital deals exclusively in apartment loans. We have closed loans on A B & C and have specialized in B & C properties as well as TIC financing. Provident Capital is proud to have an established network of commercial real estate relationships and a proven ability to structure complex loans with low transaction expenses. Having closed over $575 million in loans in 14 states, Provident Capital is very well connected with national, regional and local funding sources including, but not limited to, agency lenders (e.g. Fannie Mae, Freddie Mac), commercial banks. We look forward to working you.

Financial & Operational Highlights:

  • Responsible for credit underwriting/valuation of commercial loans

About Tracy

Tracy brings more than seventeen years of experience in Multi Family Lending. When you work with Tracy, he will oversee the loan from application, and origination, through processing and closing. He has closed over 159 transactions totaling $575 Million in loans with over 850 clients. Tracy successfully structured complex loans with as many as 35 individual borrowers per loan. Tracy will navigate relationships from legal counsel to third party vendors to streamline the process for you.


On a more personal note, Tracy earned his Bachelor degree from the University of Utah and his Master of Business Administration from Brigham Young University. He enjoys traveling, live music at concerts, a good Diet Coke, and gardening -especially tomatoes and peppers for a mean salsa.
